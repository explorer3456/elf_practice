Why Learn the details of ELF?
=============================

-> Generic understanding of how an operating system works
	It helps to learn the inner workings of our operating system

-> Development of software
	When something goes wrong, we might better understand what happened (or why)

-> Digital Forensics and Incident Response (DFIR)
	Value of able to research ELF files, especially after a security breach or discover suspicious files

-> Malware research (Binary Analysis)
